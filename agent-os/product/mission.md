# Product Mission

## Problem

School administration in Bangladesh is still largely paper-based, spreadsheet-driven, or fragmented across disconnected tools. Schools struggle to manage attendance, fee collection, academic records, HR, and communications in a unified way — resulting in errors, delays, and administrative overload for staff.

## Target Users

- **School Staff** (primary): School admins, sub-admins, teachers, and office staff at Bangladeshi educational institutions (Nursery through Class 12). They perform daily operational tasks — attendance, fee collection, mark entry, timetable management — and need a fast, reliable tool that reduces manual work.
- **Super Admins / Platform Operators**: The SaaS operator team who manages multiple school tenants, subscriptions, billing, and platform-level configuration across the system.

## Solution

NEXA EduManage is a multi-tenant SaaS school management platform built specifically for the Bangladeshi education system. It unifies every operational department — academics, admissions, finance, HR, exams, communications, and auxiliary services — into a single bilingual (English/Bangla) platform. Unlike generic school software, it is localized for Bangladesh: BDT currency, DD/MM/YYYY date format, Bangla script support, and workflows aligned to how Bangladeshi schools actually operate.
