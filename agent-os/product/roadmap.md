# Product Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP

Core operational modules needed for a school to run daily on the platform:

- **Authentication & RBAC** — Multi-role login (Super Admin, Admin, Teacher, Staff) with permission-based access control
- **Student Management & Admissions** — Enrolment, profiles, class assignment, admission workflows
- **Daily Attendance** — Teacher-facing attendance marking per class/subject, admin overview reports
- **Fee Collection & Basic Finance** — Fee structure setup, collection recording, receipts, basic ledger
- **Class / Subject / Timetable Management** — Class structure, subject assignment, weekly timetable builder
- **Homework & Assignments** — Assignment creation, submission tracking, teacher feedback
- **Basic Dashboard & Reporting** — Role-appropriate dashboards with key operational metrics

## Phase 2: Post-Launch

Extended modules to complete the full school management suite:

- **Payroll & HR Management** — Staff salary, leave management, loan tracking, performance appraisals
- **Examination & Results Management** — Exam scheduling, mark entry, result sheet generation, merit lists
- **Library Module** — Book inventory, issue/return tracking, fine management
- **Transport Module** — Route management, vehicle tracking, student bus assignment
- **Hostel Module** — Room allocation, hostel fee, warden management
- **Canteen & Inventory** — Stock management, canteen billing, inventory tracking
- **Communications** — In-app notices, SMS notifications, event calendar, parent announcements
- **Multi-School / Multi-Branch Support** — Branch management under one school account
- **Mobile App (Parents & Students)** — View attendance, results, fee status, homework, and notices
- **Advanced Analytics & Custom Reports** — Custom report builder, trend analysis, exportable data
